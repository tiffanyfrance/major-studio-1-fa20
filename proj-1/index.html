An Analysis of Topic Tagging of Portraits by Gender

This visualization shows topics by gender from four Smithsonian Institute units: the National Museum of American History, the American Art Museum, the Portrait Gallery, and the National Museum of African American History and Culture. Three most popular female and male names were searched in the API: Mary, Elizabeth, Anna and James, John, William - name popuarity as reported from the Social Security Administration (and other sources).
