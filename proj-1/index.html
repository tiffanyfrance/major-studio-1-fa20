USING SMITHSONIAN INSTITUTION OPEN API

An Analysis of Topic Tagging by Gender

This visualization shows topics by gender as tagged in the collections from four Smithsonian Institute units: the National Museum of American History, the American Art Museum, the Portrait Gallery, and the National Museum of African American History and Culture. Three female and three male names have been put into the Open API to retrieve items. The selected names were the most popular by gender, from the 1800s to late 1900s: Mary, Elizabeth, Anna and James, John, William as reported on the the Social Security Administration website (and other sources).
